Welcome to the Recycling Game!

Your goal? Put garbage in the correct trash bin.
Reminder about the recycling rules in Paris:
Yellow trash bin for items that can be RECYCLED
like plastic bottles, cans, paper...
Green trash bin for items that CANNOT be RECYCLED
Be careful: You need to get all garbage!

Here are the rules:

+1
If you put the waste in the correct trash bin 👏🏼
-1
If you put the waste in the wrong trash bin 👎🏼
-2
